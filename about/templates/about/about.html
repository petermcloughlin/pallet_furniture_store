Who Are We?

The Pallet Furniture Store is an established woodworking business, that specialises in the design, build and delivery of high quality furnishings. We have a team of woodwork specialists who design and deliver a high level of quality of both home and garden furniture pieces to suit your taste.

What do We Do?

Here at the Pallet Furniture Store, we liase with a number of builder providers and construction companies, nationwide to collect, refurbish and re-cycle both, used and un-used pallets to produce bespoke furniture pieces, that are built to a high standard, while protecting the environment from unwanted waste.
